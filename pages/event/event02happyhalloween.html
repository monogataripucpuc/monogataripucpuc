New Event! Happy Halloween!

October 19 16:00 to October 31 23:59
Exchange deadline is November 7 23:59
Requirement to participate is clearing Hitagi Club 01-003
Collect "Sweets" and "Pumpkins" in exchange for Event-Only Scenes and other items at the exchange place


How to get Sweets and Pumpkins

The Event is made up of 5 Event Maps and 2 Bonus Maps.
For every stage you are required to use a PucPuc with a specific trait which could be something simple like "Use Koyomi" or more specific like "Use someone with a headgear".
The Event PucPucs and Scenes give bonuses to the rewards.
There are two Bonus stages that are available at the top of the Event Map.
The red Bonus stage is available at all times and should be ignored until all stages are finished.
The blue Bonus stage can pop up at random and should be done immediately.
